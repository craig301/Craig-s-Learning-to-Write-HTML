Our Trip to Yellowstone

Exploring Yellowstone with Friends

At the end of April, Paul and Moriah Chambers, Craig and Grace Blotter, and Keenan and Susanna Howard drove over 2,00 miles to visit Yellowstone National Park. Along the way, they visited the Badlands, Black Hills, and Tetons. Their trip passed them through six states, including Iowa, Nebraska, South Dakota, Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho. The whole group at the West Entrance of Yellowstone.

Things they saw on their trip.

  1. A Grizzly bear
  2. Elk
  3. Pronghorn
  4. Bison
  5. Mule deer
  6. Geysers
  7. Hot springs
  8. Mountains
