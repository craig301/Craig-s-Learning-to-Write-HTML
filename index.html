Spring 2022 Trip to Yellowstone

Exploring Yellowstone with Friends

At the end of April, we drove over 2,000 miles to visit Yellowstone National Park. Along the way, we visited the Badlands, Black Hills, and Tetons. Our trip took us through six states, including Iowa, Nebraska, South Dakota, Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho.
The whole group at the West Entrance of Yellowstone.
